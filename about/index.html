About

We are EVE consulting, a broad consultancy and data management and visualisation company with a proud history. We are three committed colleagues developing questionnaires, highly sophisticated code and visual front and back end design through digital and analogue aids. We are the only operator in Norway, the largest on-shore operator in Europe, and a steadily growing force globally. Driven by our dedication to safe and provident design solutions, we always ensure the best solutions possible whilst maintaining an acceptable standard within gender equality and data privacy. We are shaping the future of online data gathering, fabulous plotting and odd data correlation plots.
